Pace

One approach to spread a crucial message is through presentation. BeMe Simulation monitors your speaking tempo because:


  • Studies show that speaking too quickly reduces listener understanding and message retention.
  • Speaking too slowly might cause the listeners to lose interest in what you are saying, which can harm their ability to understand and remember what you are saying.

Recommendations:


  • Before starting a new slide or segment of your presentation, take a deep breath.
  • Try to think of a good transitional phrase you can use when you switch to the next slide, even if it's something as straightforward as, "Let's move on," or "Let's talk about (the subject of the next slide)." As you go along, this may assist you to relax.
  • Be aware that speaking quickly or excitedly due to your love for the subiect vou are discussing may lead your listeners to lose interest in what you are saying.


Filler words

Confidence enhances the persuasiveness of your message when you are telling a narrative to an audience. BeMe Simulation listens for filler words because:


  • According to research, an audience may feel less trust in a presenter's knowledge If they utilize filler words frequently. In turn, this may cause the audience to have less faith in the tale being conveyed.
  • Although utilizing filler words is a common speaking technique, if you use too many, they may detract from your point or worse. As you use this tool over time, Speaker Coach will gain knowledge.

Recommendations:


  • If you need a moment to collect your thoughts, pause.
  • Take a deep breath to calm yourself down if you are anxious.
  • Make time to practice more before the presentation to become more familiar with your subject.
  • If you frequently find yourself utilizing filler words to signal transitions, make a list of suitable transitional phrases.

Pitch

Speaking in monotone can make it more difficult for the target audience to stay engaged or recognize what to focus on.


Recommendations:


In order to keep their interest and enhance your communication be positive to emphasize the key words and phrases by means of adjusting your quantity and pitch. If you are working from notes or a script be cautious not to just read from the script as that can lead you to be extra monotone and much less natural.



Pronunciation

Recommendations:


  • Be clear and precise in how you pronounce your words. If there are words that are challenging for you to pronounce correctly, practice them carefully until you are assured that you can say them besides tripping up.
